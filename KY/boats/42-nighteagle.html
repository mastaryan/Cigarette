42' Nighteagle

The 42' Nighteagle exemplifies Cigarette's determination to continuously innovate and expand its horizons. We created a lifestyle powerboat by merging a performance hull with a peninsula style deck and cabin. The efficiency of the Nighteagle hull propels the boat to exhilarating speeds and the first Mercury certified five-engine application will make it a stand-out at any dock!

ADVANCED HELM

The Nighteagle’s advanced helm features three 17” Garmin multifunctional displays flush mounted to a state-of-the-art dash assembly milled from a solid block of aluminum that is anodized and hard coated for durability. It includes integrated cup holders, glovebox compartments, stainless steel grab bars and ergonomic CNC machined footrests to provide convenience, safety and comfort that is synonymous with Cigarette.

ENTERTAINMENT ON THE WATER

At 42' long, and a 11’ 11” beam, this is a Cigarette with extra room to spare above and below deck. Enjoy luxury day boating with ample space to entertain friends and family. The detailed design and construction of the Nighteagle create the ultimate comfort-focused Cigarette with speed, technology, and luxurious space. The Nighteagle boasts a commanding presence on the water and at the dock.

CONVERTIBLE LOUNGE

Aft forward and rear facing seats electronically extend to form an oversized sun lounge for the ultimate mix of comfort and style.

DIGITAL SWITCHING AND AUTOMATION

Cigarette Racing’s meticulously engineered and designed digital switching system allows you to control almost any function of the 42’ Nighteagle directly from the Garmin multifunctional displays. An automatic sequential engine system activates all engines with a simple press of a button.

OPTIONAL ADJUSTABLE HELM SEATING

The ergonomic helm seating is constructed with multi-density foam for comfort both at dock and underway. Each of the three electric forward helm seats are independently adjustable allowing for different height programming to suit individual comfort preference. Additionally, the seat bottoms can flip up to convert the standard seating into bolsters.

LIGHTING WITHOUT LIMITS

The Nighteagle uses RGB ambient lighting that can be digitally controlled with multifunctional Garmin displays. Powered LED lighting with infinite color variations is featured throughout the deck including high powered down lights in the hardtop and red captains lighting. The technology extends seamlessly into an underwater RGB system to create the perfect ambiance for a night out on the water.

JL AUDIO X CIGARETTE RACING

For the finest marine sound systems, Cigarette relies on the leader in the industry, JL Audio Marine. Their products meet Cigarette’s pressing demand for audiophile quality systems that can withstand the rigors of the wind, sea and open cockpits. These sound systems are tested on each model to ensure they are appropriate for the boat size and seating configuration in order to provide the ultimate Cigarette Racing entertainment experience.

GARMIN X CIGARETTE RACING

As the first manufacturer to be Garmin-Certified, Cigarette Racing has proudly partnered with Garmin to feature state-of-the-art technology and design to provide our owners with the most ergonomic and intuitive resources to make a day on the water easy, fun, and safe. The optional Garmin Convenience package places a 9” multifunctional display at the aft conversational area and bow section for additional control points of digital switching, media, and navigation. No need to get up, just tap the screen!

CARBON FIBER HARDTOP

Our exclusive hardtop was skillfully engineered to provide a lower center of gravity for increased performance and comfort without compromising a beautiful design aesthetic. The four-pillar, all carbon fiber hardtop features a wide, double-curvature, frameless clear laminated safety glass windscreen offering exceptional protection from the elements. The hardtop includes integrated flood lights, boarding handrails, full-spectrum ambient lighting, electrically actuated sunshade, and optional windshield wipers with a cleaning system.

SUNSHADE

The Nighteagle’s hardtop offers an optional electrically actuated sunshade. At the press of a button, the shade will cover the aft entertainment area. All done and ready to get underway? Simply press the ”shade in” button on your Garmin display and it will retract into the hardtop.

COMFORT AND LAYOUT

The Nighteagle features multi-layered foams coupled with detailed quilted patterns using Cigarette “Cool Touch” Fabric, a heat resistant fabric for premium comfort. Powered forward helm seats are independently adjustable and quickly convert from standard seating to standing bolsters for convenience and drive comfort in all conditions. Guests enjoy aft forward and rear facing seats that electronically extend to form an oversized sun lounge for the ultimate mix of comfort and style.

PENINSULA STYLE WALK AROUND

A walk-around configuration gives a yacht-like feel. On the port side, a door leads to the fully accessorized cabin below and in the front deck, an ergonomic chaise invites lounging.

CABIN

The Nighteagle features a spacious cabin with CNC cut cabinets and Corian countertops. Additionally, the 7+ foot headroom provides surprising freedom of movement.
